Item #: SCP-7814
Level 2 — Restricted
Object Class: SAFE (Conditional)

Special Containment Procedures

SCP-7814 is contained as an anomalous artifact under the custodianship of PoI-7814-1 (Tsougkouris Okkotsis), employed as a containment specialist at Site-45, located in Fterikoudi, Cyprus.

SCP-7814 is to remain in PoI-7814-1’s possession at all times. Transfer of SCP-7814 to other individuals has demonstrated no anomalous effects and is not recommended unless authorized by Site Command for testing purposes.

No attempts are to be made to forcibly reactivate SCP-7814 or induce manifestation of the associated anomalous entity (designated SCP-7814-A) without Level ██ approval.

Psychological monitoring of PoI-7814-1 is mandatory, with emphasis on stress response, identity displacement, and familial-role inferiority.

Description

SCP-7814 is a metal ring of unknown composition, formerly utilized as a containment and control medium for an anomalous entity designated SCP-7814-A [informally referred to in archived documentation as “Rika”].

SCP-7814-A is confirmed to have been created through unknown means by Georgios Okkotsis, former head of the Gojo Clan and a long-term anomalous asset affiliated with the Foundation. During Georgios Okkotsis’ lifetime, SCP-7814-A was entirely stable, non-hostile, and responded exclusively to his direct commands via ██ ██ ██████ ██ ████████ █████. No containment breaches or deviations were recorded during this period.

SCP-7814-A’s compliance parameters appear to have been uniquely attuned to Georgios Okkotsis. No other individual has successfully interacted with or activated SCP-7814-A due to ██ ███████ █████████ ████ ███████.

Addendum 7814-1: Succession Record

Prior to his death from natural causes, Georgios Okkotsis personally gifted SCP-7814 to his granddaughter, Georgia Okkotsis, as a familial keepsake and symbol of trust. At the time of transfer, SCP-7814 had already entered a state of reduced anomalous activity.

Following Georgios Okkotsis’s death, SCP-7814 became completely inert. No manifestations, responses, or detectable energy signatures associated with SCP-7814-A were recorded.

Several months later, the Gojo Clan intervened and reassigned SCP-7814 from Georgia Okkotsis to her younger brother, Tsougkouris Okkotsis, citing internal evaluations classified under ██-██-██ that deemed him weaker than his sister in combat.

Despite objections from both siblings, the Gojo Clan insisted that SCP-7814 remain with Tsougkouris Okkotsis, designating it a protective charm rather than an operational artifact.

Addendum 7814-2: Anomalous Effects on Pol-7814-1

SCP-7814 exhibits no anomalous properties when worn by individuals outside the Okkotsis bloodline.

However, while in possession of SCP-7814, Tsougkouris Okkotsis demonstrates minor but repeatable deviations from human baseline, including:

- Elevated physical endurance during containment incidents

- Improved reaction timing under stress

- Short-term strength amplification during high-risk scenarios

These effects remain passive and limited in scope. SCP-7814-A has not manifested, communicated, or exerted independent agency.

Current Foundation theory attributes these effects to residual imprinting and biological compatibility unique to Georgios Okkotsis’s direct descendants via ██ ███████ ██ █████████ ██ ██████.

Addendum 7814-3: Employment at Site-45

Tsougkouris Okkotsis is currently employed as a containment specialist at Site-45, Fterikoudi, following Ethics Committee approval under Protocol ██-██.

The committee’s ruling states: “Continued proximity between PoI-7814-1 and SCP-7814 represents the most stable and lowest-risk containment configuration currently available.”

PoI-7814-1 has not been briefed on SCP-7814-A’s prior operational role beyond general archival summaries and has never witnessed manifestation activity.

Addendum 7814-4: Risk Assessment

SCP-7814 remains classified as Safe due to its current inert state. However, reclassification to Neutralized has been denied due to the following factors:

- Confirmed persistence of SCP-7814-A within the artifact

- Absence of a known reactivation method due to ██ █████████ ███████

- Genetic dependency on the Okkotsis lineage

Historical precedent of SCP-7814-A’s effectiveness under Georgios Okkotsis remains ██ █████████.

Predictive models indicate that SCP-7814-A, if reactivated, may not retain prior obedience parameters under ██ ███████ █████████.

Closing Statement

SCP-7814 is dormant. It is not neutralized. It remains where containment is most stable, in the hands of someone who was never meant to carry it.
